# 08_LEGAL_AND_COMPLIANCE_POSTURE

**Status:** CANONICAL  
**Version:** v0.1

## Purpose
Protects the product and its operators from liability while respecting the rights of content owners.

## Hard Constraints
*   **Fair Use limits:** We display only headlines and short snippets (thumbnails). We never scrape/display full articles.
*   **Link-Out Model:** The primary action is always driving traffic to the publisher.
*   **Paywall Respect:** We identify paywalled content (e.g., via icon) but do not bypass it.
*   **Defamation Avoidance:** We generally avoid republishing defamatory claims in system voice; we quote them if they are the news event itself.
*   **Opt-Out:** If a publisher formally requests removal, we comply immediately pending review.

## Explicit Non-Goals
*   **No "Reader View":** We do not build a reading mode that effectively strips ads/revenue from the publisher.
*   **No Archive Service:** We are not a permanent record; we are a discovery engine.
