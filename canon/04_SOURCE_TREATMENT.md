# 04_SOURCE_TREATMENT

**Status:** CANONICAL  
**Version:** v0.1

## Purpose
Establishes the rules for how external media outlets are represented, credited, and treated within the system.

## Hard Constraints
*   **Equal Platform:** All admitted sources are treated with identical UI hierarchy (size, font weight, positioning logic).
*   **Mandatory Attribution:** Every headline, snippet, or claim displayed must differ visually from system text and explicitly cite the source.
*   **Logo Usage:** Source logos are used strictly for identification, not decoration.
*   **Independence:** The Product accepts no payment for placement or favorable treatment of any source.
*   **Direct Linking:** Users must always be able to navigate to the original source material.

## Explicit Non-Goals
*   **No "Preferred Partners":** We do not boost sources based on business relationships.
*   **No Quality Grading:** We do not display "reliability scores" or "trust ratings" next to sources. We let the comparison speak.
*   **No Endorsement:** Inclusion of a source does not constitute endorsement of its content or worldview.
