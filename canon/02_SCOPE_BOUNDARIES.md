# 02_SCOPE_BOUNDARIES

**Status:** CANONICAL  
**Version:** v0.1

## Purpose
Delineates the strict operational boundaries of the product regarding geography, media types, and language support.

## Hard Constraints
*   **Primary Geography:** Israel, The West Bank, Gaza, and immediate borders (Lebanon/Syria/Egypt/Jordan borders).
*   **Secondary Geography:** Global events with *direct* impact on Israel (e.g., US support bills, UN resolutions regarding Israel).
*   **Media Scope:** Established, professional news outlets (digital press, TV, radio).
*   **Language Scope - Input:** Hebrew, English, Arabic.
*   **Language Scope - Output:** Hebrew (exclusive for v0.1).
*   **Source Threshold:** Sources must have an editorial board or identifiable journalistic structure.

## Explicit Non-Goals
*   **No Global General News:** US domestic politics, Eurozone economy, etc., are out of scope unless directly tied to Israel.
*   **No Social Media:** Twitter/X, Telegram, TikTok, and Facebook are excluded as ingestible sources.
*   **No Pure Opinion Blogs:** Individual substacks or opinion-only sites are excluded.
