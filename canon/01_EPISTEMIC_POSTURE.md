# 01_EPISTEMIC_POSTURE

**Status:** CANONICAL  
**Version:** v0.1

## Purpose
Defines strictly how the system handles truth, claims, and uncertainty, determining what can be asserted by the machine.

## Hard Constraints
*   **Attributed Reality:** The product does not state "X happened." It states "Source Y reports X happened."
*   **Uncertainty as First-Class:** When sources disagree, the disagreement is the primary data point.
*   **No Intent Attribution:** We never claim to know *why* a source framed a story a certain way (e.g., "to hide the truth").
*   **Descriptive, Not Judgmental:** Use terms like "omitted," "emphasized," or "focused on," rather than "censored," "biased," or "distorted."
*   **Unknowns:** It is acceptable and required to state "The details are currently unclear" rather than synthesizing a likely reality.

## Explicit Non-Goals
*   **No "Debunking" Label:** We do not verify facts ourselves. We surface conflicting reports.
*   **No "Bias" Scores:** We do not assign numerical bias ratings to outlets or articles.
*   **No Moralizing:** We do not evaluate the morality of the news or the coverage.
