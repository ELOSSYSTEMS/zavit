# 00_PRODUCT_DEFINITION

**Status:** CANONICAL  
**Version:** v0.1

## Purpose
Defines the product’s immutable identity, the core user problem it solves, and the hard boundaries of what it will never become.

## Hard Constraints
*   **Core Function:** The Product is a news comparison and clustering engine, not a publisher.
*   **Atomic Unit:** The Product clusters **Events**, not independent articles.
*   **Geo-Focus:** The Product is exclusively Israel-focused.
*   **Language & UI:** The experience is Hebrew-first and RTL-native.
*   **Epistemic Role:** The Product acts as a mirror to the media landscape, not an arbiter of absolute truth.
*   **Neutrality:** The Product must never express an opinion, take a stance, or advocate for a specific outcome.

## Explicit Non-Goals
*   **No Original Reporting:** We do not employ journalists or break news.
*   **No Social Media Aggregation:** We do not index tweets, posts, or influencer content as primary sources.
*   **No "Feed" Optimization:** We do not optimize for engagement or time-on-site via algorithmic feedstock.
*   **Not a Truth Checker:** We do not tag stories as "False" or "True"; we show where they differ.
